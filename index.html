Initial Consultation

2

University & Program Selection

Our experts match you with suitable universities and programs in Turkey, Libya, or other destinations.

Comprehensive briefing about your destination country, culture, and what to expect upon arrival.

6

Post-Arrival Support

Continued assistance with accommodation, registration, and settling into your new environment.

About External Education

Founded in Egypt with a passion for empowering students, External Education has been guiding Egyptian students to international education opportunities since 2010.

Our mission is to break down barriers to quality education by connecting talented Egyptian students with world-class universities in Turkey, Libya, and other countries.

With a team of experienced advisors who have personally navigated the challenges of studying abroad, we understand the unique needs of Egyptian students and provide tailored guidance every step of the way.

We specialize in placements for engineering, medicine, business, and other fields at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels.
